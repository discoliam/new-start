Registered Dietitians are uniquely qualified to translate scientific information about food into practical dietary advice. The majority of dietitians work within a hospital setting or supporting people in their own home whose quality of life is linked closely with their diet.

Dietitians also plan a significant role in the following areas; the food industry, public health, education, sports nutrition, research or the media and a growing number decide to work in a freelance capacity.
